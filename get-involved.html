Veyon is a community project and lives from contributions all over the world. Here are just a few things you can do to help and grow our community:

  Make a donation to the project

  Contribute to our community forum and support other Veyon users

  Create or improve translations for your language

  Write an article on Veyon for either your or our blog

  Create a video on installing or using Veyon

  Help with coding
