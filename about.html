  1. Veyon is the most widely used OpenSource classroom management software with more than 100,000 downloads per year.
  2. We commit ourselves to the idea of free and OpenSource software to support students and educational instituations around the world.
  3. Veyon Solutions – based in Chemnitz/Germany – is the company driving forward the development of Veyon and offering additional commercial solutions and services.
  4. The community behind Veyon is made up of dozens of volunteers who contribute their time and skills for the project.
  5. Our history started in 2004 with the well-known predecessor software iTALC. Our roadmap for Veyon is publicly available.
